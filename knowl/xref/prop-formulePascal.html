Proposition 4.2.18.
Soit \(n,\ r\in \N\) tels que \(0 \lt r \leq n\text{,}\) alors
\begin{equation*} \Binomial{n}{r}=\Binomial{n-1}{r-1} + \Binomial{n-1}{r}. \end{equation*}
